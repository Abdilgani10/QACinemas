BBFC Film Ratings
Universal: Suitable for audiences aged four years and over.
Parental Guidance: Unaccompanied children of any age may watch, but some scenes may be unsuitable for children under 8 years old.
Suitable for 12 years and over, adult supervision advised: Films classified 12A contain material that is not generally suitable for children aged under 12. No one younger than 12 may see a 12A film in a cinema unless accompanied by an adult.
Suitable for 12 years and over: No one younger than 12 may see a 12 film in a cinema.
Suitable only for 15 years and over: No one younger than 15 may see a 15 film in a cinema.
Suitable only for adults: No one younger than 18 may see a 18 film in a cinema.
To be shown only in specially licensed cinemas: The R18 category is a special and legally-restricted classification primarily for explicit works of consenting sex or strong fetish material involving adults. Films may only be shown to adults in specially licensed cinemas.
